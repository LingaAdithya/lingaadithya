## the short version

Full stack developer and AI engineer, freelance, based in the "ship it and figure the rest out" school of building. I take ideas that exist as a voice note or a napkin sketch and turn them into software that real people use.

No fluff, no "revolutionizing the industry" energy — just someone who's genuinely into:

- Backend engineering that doesn't fall over at 2am
- Full stack development, front to back, no gaps
- Artificial intelligence and machine learning that solves an actual problem
- Computer vision, because teaching machines to see is still wild to me
- System design — the puzzle I never get tired of
- Product development, from "what if" to "it's live"

I've spent the last few years working with founders, small teams, and businesses — taking their idea through the entire pipeline: requirements, architecture, build, deploy, repeat.
